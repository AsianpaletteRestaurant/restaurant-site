Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

Nestled in the vibrant culinary scene of Dublin, Asian Palette has been serving rich, flavorful Indian cuisine since 2010. As a family-owned restaurant with deep roots in traditional Indian cooking, we bring the bold spices, aromatic herbs, and time-honored recipes of India to the heart of Ireland. From sizzling tandoori grills to creamy butter chicken and hearty biryanis, every dish is crafted with authenticity, care, and a passion for flavor. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere, Asian Palette has become a local favorite for both dine-in and takeaway. Whether you're seeking a comforting curry, vegetarian delights, or a spicy culinary adventure, we invite you to spice up your life with our handcrafted Indian delicacies.
